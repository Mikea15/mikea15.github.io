Jumpy Rope is an endless arcade jumper game for iOS and Android platforms, featuring low poly graphic style and simple gameplay mechanics. Jump the rope on increasingly difficult floating island environments that you can unlock, time your jumps perfectly to win extra coins and beat your friend's scores on cross-platforms leaderboards.

Play against your friends by connecting to facebook. Jumpy Rope features character customization, and has Powerups to help you go further.
